ABOUT GABCEE Airbnb & APARTMENT

Welcome to GABCEE Airbnb located in a serene environment in Lagos State, we're more than just a place to stay - we're a home away from home. Whether you're visiting for work, adventure, or relaxation, our goal is to make your stay comfortable, memorable, and stress-free.

At Gabcee Airbnb, we believe that travel should be about experiences, not just accommodations. That's why we've designed our space with your comfort in mind. From cozy furnishings to thoughtful touches, every detail has been carefully curated to ensure you feel at ease the moment you step through the door.

what makes us special

Location: We are located 15 minutes drive from Lagos State International Airport. Whether you want to explore the city, enjoy the local culture, or relax in a quiet corner of town, you'll find everything just minutes away.

Comfort Meets Style: Our apartment combines modern design with warm, welcoming vibes. Expect premium bedding, a fully equipped kitchen, high-speed WiFi, and a living space that's perfect for unwinding after a long day.

A Personal Touch: We take pride in going the extra mile for our guests. Need recommendations for restaurants, events, or hidden gems? We're happy to share local tips to help you make the most of your stay.

Peace of Mind: Your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. From sparkling-clean spaces to a smooth check-in process, we've thought of everything to ensure your visit is worry-free.

We are passionate about serving you and look forward to having you.
